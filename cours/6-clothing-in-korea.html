Conclusion

Korean clothing has evolved from its traditional roots to embrace modern fashion while maintaining cultural significance. The transformation reflects broader societal changes, from strict social hierarchies to contemporary individual expression. Understanding this evolution provides insight into Korean society's modernization while preserving cultural heritage.

The balance between traditional values and modern fashion continues to shape Korean clothing culture, creating a unique fusion that influences both domestic and international fashion trends.
